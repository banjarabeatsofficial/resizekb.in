About ResizeKB

ResizeKB is a free online image resize and compression tool. It helps users compress images under specific sizes like 20KB, 50KB, 100KB and 200KB.

This tool is useful for online forms, exams, job applications, documents and other image upload requirements.

ResizeKB works directly in your browser. Your images are not uploaded to any server.
